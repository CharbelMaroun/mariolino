About us

A magical pop-up experience created in the guest house, Villa Paradiso.

Located in Batroun’s Old Souks, Mariolino is now known as “the hidden gem” of the area, offering a combination of the freshest salads and grills and a selection of the best wood-fired pizzas in town, where all wines are offered exclusively from Batroun’s local vineyards.

In short, Mariolino is a place of good food, magic and endless charm.
